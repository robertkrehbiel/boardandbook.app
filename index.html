The Experience

Every position from a real game.
Every puzzle from chess history.

Board & Book isn't another chess app. It's a curated collection - where every position you study was played by a real grandmaster in a real tournament. No anonymous games. Just the most beautiful, instructive moments in chess history, presented with the care they deserve.

Three Rooms

I

The Board

Play

Face the Arasan engine, a tournament-strength opponent that adapts to your level. Challenge friends or match with online opponents. Full post-game analysis after every match.

II

The Book

Learn

Walk through chess history like chapters in a book. The board updates as you read and every move is tied to the story of the game.

III

Tactics

Train

Solve puzzles drawn from historic games. They are organized by theme, difficulty, and era to offer the user an exploratory learning session that will not only improve their skills, but teach them the history of this elegant game. Additionally, play the daily puzzles to sharpen your skills.

Chess is not merely a game. It is a living history — every move a chapter, every game a story worth preserving.
